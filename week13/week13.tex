	Check the stability of the system $\ddot{x} + p(t)x = 0$  for a given $a$ and $b$ at the origin, where 
	
	$p(t) =  \begin{cases}
		a^2,\, 2\pi k < t < 2\pi k + \pi \\
		b^2,\, 2\pi k + \pi \leq t \leq 2\pi k + 2\pi
	\end{cases}$, $k\in\mathrm{Z}$.
